Koleksi prompt

Bahasa Indonesia

  • Sunrise Pegunungan:
    Pemandangan alam Indonesia saat matahari terbit, pegunungan berkabut di latar, hutan tropis lebat, sungai jernih berkelok di foreground, cahaya keemasan yang menembus kabut, komposisi wide-angle, detail dedaunan, suasana damai dan magis.
  • Hutan & Fauna:
    Hutan hujan tropis dengan kanopi lebat, burung eksotis hinggap di dahan, kupu-kupu terbang, sinar matahari menembus celah daun, tekstur lembap, warna hijau kaya, atmosfer natural.
  • Pantai Sunset:
    Pantai eksotis dengan ombak lembut, langit jingga keemasan saat senja, garis horizon jelas, refleksi cahaya di permukaan air, suasana tenang dan kontemplatif, format sinematik.
  • Air Terjun Tropis:
    Air terjun bertingkat di lembah hijau, kabut halus di sekitar jatuhan air, bebatuan berlumut, tanaman pakis di sekitar, kontras antara putih air dan hijau hutan, komposisi leading lines.

English

  • Mountain sunrise:
    Indonesian landscape at sunrise with misty mountains, dense tropical rainforest, a meandering clear river in the foreground, golden light piercing through the mist, wide-angle composition, detailed foliage, serene and magical mood.
  • Tropical wildlife:
    Rainforest canopy with rich greenery, an exotic bird perched on a branch, butterflies fluttering, sunlight filtering through leaves, moist textures, vibrant greens, authentic natural atmosphere.
  • Beach sunset:
    Exotic beach with gentle waves, golden-orange sky at dusk, clear horizon line, light reflections on the water surface, contemplative and calm mood, cinematic framing.
  • Tropical waterfall:
    Tiered waterfall in a lush valley, soft mist around the cascade, mossy rocks, ferns surrounding the area, contrast between white water and deep greens, leading line composition.

Pascaproduksi

  • Format: 9:16, 1:1, 16:9 untuk kanal distribusi
  • Caption: Edukasi, konservasi, call-to-action
  • Publikasi: Sosial media, blog, presentasi
